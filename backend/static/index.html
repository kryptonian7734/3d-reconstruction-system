Why multiple viewpoints?

3D reconstruction works by triangulating common features across images. More overlapping viewpoints = better depth estimation. Aim for 10-50 images captured in a 360° arc around your object.

Why does reconstruction take time?

COLMAP performs computationally intensive operations: detecting thousands of features per image, matching them across all pairs, solving camera geometry, and computing depth maps. This ensures high-quality results comparable to professional photogrammetry software.

      What affects 3D quality?

      Model quality depends on: (1) Image sharpness and resolution, (2) Consistent lighting without harsh shadows, (3) Textured surfaces with distinctive features, (4) Sufficient overlap between consecutive images (60-80% recommended), and (5) Stable camera positions.
